The Backend

Understand the magic that happens behind the modularity of our interactive GUI.

Collection Our PyQT5 GUI collects your presentation and enables you to configure your slideshow.
Extraction Python's pptx module extracts the requisite data and makes it available for computation.
Synchronization Python's NLP toolkit aids in speech recognition in real time as users present.
Computation Our similarity algorithm parses data from the two sources and controls flow.
Iteration The processes repeat automatically, enabling you to present hassle-free.
Conclusion The backend processes end as soon as the last slide is reached. You're done!
